## Overview

SkillMatch AI is a full-stack AI-powered platform that helps students evaluate their resumes, identify skill gaps, and discover relevant internship opportunities.

The platform analyzes uploaded resumes, extracts technical skills, generates AI-powered insights, and recommends career improvement paths. The long-term vision is to create a centralized internship discovery ecosystem where students can find opportunities, improve their profiles, and track applications from one place.

## Problem Statement

Students often spend hours searching across multiple internship portals and struggle to understand whether their skills match job requirements.

SkillMatch AI aims to solve this problem by:

* Centralizing internship discovery
* Analyzing resumes automatically
* Identifying missing skills
* Providing actionable career insights
* Reducing the time spent searching manually
